About Us

Yousalama is an initiative that aims to improve security levels in residential areas and private sectors by leveraging the power of neural networks, artificial intelligence and Internet of Things. Our solution leverages the possibility of AI working on machines and machine learning frameworks using the state of the art technology architecture.

Our vision is to provide a sustainable, efficient and affordable method of using less and encrypted data to enhance in each and every building.
